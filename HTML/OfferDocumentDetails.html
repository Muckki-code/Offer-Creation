Offer Document Creation: Details & Possibilities

The application automates the generation of professional offer documents, ensuring consistency and accuracy. The documents are created based on a pre-defined Google Docs template and populated with data from your Google Sheet, along with additional details provided by the AE.

Content and Structure

The generated offer document typically includes the following sections:

  • Offer Nature Description: Clearly states if it's a "binding" or "non-binding" offer.
  • Customer Information: Customer Company Name, Address, and Contact Person are dynamically inserted from the sheet's header section.
  • Offer Details: Includes the Offer Creation Date, Offer Valid Until Date, and Overall Contract Term, sourced directly from your sheet's configuration cells.
  • Everphone Contact: Your name, position, and email (as the AE) are populated, ensuring the customer knows who to reach out to.
  • Special Agreements: If the "Special Agreements" field in the sheet contains text, a dedicated section titled "Sondervereinbarungen:" (German) or "Special Agreements:" (English) will be added to the document, followed by your custom text. If the field is empty, this section will not appear.
  • Device Table: This is the core of the offer. Only devices from the sheet with an "Approved (Original Price)" or "Approved (New Price)" status will be included. The table displays:
    • Device Model: The name of the device.
    • Quantity: Number of units.
    • Term: Contract term in months.
    • Net Monthly Rental Price: The approved monthly price per device.
    • Total Net Monthly Rental Price: Calculated as Quantity * Approved Price, showing the total monthly cost for that device line item.
    • Grand Total: The sum of all "Total Net Monthly Rental Price" for all approved devices.

Template Management and Localization

The application uses separate Google Docs templates for English and German offers, allowing for localization. The template used is determined by the "Language" setting in your Google Sheet.

  • Template IDs: Defined in config.gs (CONFIG.templates.english and CONFIG.templates.german).
  • Placeholder Replacement: The system automatically finds and replaces placeholders (e.g., {{CustomerCompanyName}}) in the chosen template with the actual data from your sheet.

Document Possibilities and Benefits

  • Automation: Eliminates manual copy-pasting, reducing errors and saving time.
  • Consistency: Ensures all offers adhere to a standard format and include all necessary information.
  • Approval Integration: Directly links document generation to the approval workflow, ensuring only finalized and approved offers are created.
  • Audit Trail: Every document creation is logged, providing a clear record of when offers were generated and with what data.
  • Customization: While automated, the content is dynamic, adapting to customer details, selected devices, and special agreements.
